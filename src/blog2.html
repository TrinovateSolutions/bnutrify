How nutrition supports hormonal health

Nutrition plays a crucial role in supporting hormone production, insulin sensitivity and menstrual health. The right food patterns reduce inflammation and support balance.

Key nutrients & foods

  • Fiber: legumes, vegetables — supports estrogen balance
  • Omega-3: flax, fatty fish — reduces inflammation
  • Magnesium & Zinc: nuts, seeds, whole grains — supports mood and cycles

PCOS-friendly tips

Focus on low-GI carbs, include protein at every meal, and emphasise strength training alongside diet to improve insulin sensitivity.

Recipe idea: Moong dal chilla stuffed with spiced paneer — protein-rich & satisfying.

Period support

Include iron-rich foods (spinach, ragi), magnesium for cramps (pumpkin seeds) and prioritize sleep & hydration.

Practical meal structure

Sample day: breakfast — oats + curd + seeds; lunch — dal + veg + brown rice; snack — nuts; dinner — grilled fish/tofu + salad.
